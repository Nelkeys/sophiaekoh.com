About Me

My name is Ekoh Sophia Joseph

I am a graduate of Medical Physiology from the University of Uyo, Akwa Ibom state, Nigeria. My expertise lies in the intersection of physiology and data analysis. I am proficient in Microsoft Excel and SQL, and story-telling which allows me to efficiently analyze and interpret complex physiological data sets and pass the information effectively. I am also a blockchain enthusiast! My drive to know more about blockchain led me to Techy Train Foundation and Know The Blocks Mavin (KBM) where I acquired relevant knowledge. I believe that blockchain technology has immense potential in revolutionizing the way we approach health data management and research. With a strong grasp of blockchain's decentralized nature, I envision secure and transparent data sharing, unlocking new possibilities in medical research and personalized healthcare. Let's collaborate to harness the power of blockchain and data-driven insights for a healthier future.
